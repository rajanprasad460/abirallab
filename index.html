OUR ORIGIN

From Agri-Tech (AgriStartup 2024)
Vision to Adaptive Robotics

The foundation of ABIRAL Lab began through an early agri-tech innovation initiative focused on intelligent agricultural mobility, adaptive mechanical systems, and sustainable robotics.

2024

ABIRAL Lab traces its origins to an agristartup initiative launched in 2024 named Project Agristartup, with a vision of developing intelligent and sustainable robotic technologies for agriculture.


The laboratory initially focused on the conceptual design and development of an E-Tractor platform integrating adaptive mobility, intelligent mechanical systems, and future-ready automation concepts.


During its early phase, the project hired its first engineer to contribute to E-Tractor design, mechanical development, and robotic system integration — marking the beginning of ABIRAL Lab’s journey into adaptive bio-inspired robotics and locomotion research.

Mission

From flowing biological motion to engineered autonomy

ABIRAL Lab aims to develop robotic systems that move, adapt, and interact intelligently. The lab emphasizes practical robotics research with strong foundations in dynamics, design, control, and computation.

Applications

  • Deep-sea inspection and intervention
  • Adaptive robotic locomotion
  • Bio-inspired underwater vehicles
  • Robotic manipulation in unstructured environments
  • Dynamics and control of multi-link systems
