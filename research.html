Research Areas

ABIRAL Lab studies adaptive robotic systems inspired by biological motion and intelligent behavior.

Bio-inspired Design

Mechanisms inspired by fish, animals, soft structures, and biological locomotion principles.

Underwater Robotics

Marine robotic systems for inspection, exploration, and manipulation in unstructured environments.

Dynamics & Control

Mathematical modeling, simulation, and control of robotic systems with complex motion.

Adaptive Locomotion

Locomotion strategies that respond to environment, task, and system constraints.

AI for Robotics

Learning, planning, perception, and decision-making for autonomous robotic platforms.

Human-Centered Robotics

Robotic technologies designed for safe, useful, and meaningful human interaction.
